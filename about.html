About Artificate



Artificate is an innovative AI-powered SaaS designed to enhance artistic creation by providing real-time feedback and improvement suggestions. Leveraging the Google Gemini API, Artificate analyzes various aspects of user-submitted artwork to offer personalized critiques and practical advice. This AI SaaS is sure to be at the forefront of the AI world, and the business opportunities present are endless. Not only is the target audience businesses, but individuals who wish to improve themselves are also present as potential customers.



"AI should better us, not be better than us."



That is the philosophy of Artificate. We want humans to learn and grow with the help of AI, and show the world another path possible for the way of artificial intelligence. Instead of AI replacing human talent, how about we make more of it?
